Lounge

Welcome to the luxurious lounge! This room is designed for relaxation and comfort, offering a cozy atmosphere for guests to unwind. The lounge is filled with plush sofas, elegant armchairs, and soft lighting that creates a tranquil ambiance.

The walls of the lounge are adorned with beautiful artwork and shelves lined with books. A grand fireplace provides warmth during colder nights, adding to the inviting atmosphere. Guests can enjoy a cup of tea or engage in engaging conversations while taking in the serene views from the large windows.

The lounge is the perfect place to take a break from the mysteries that surround the mansion. Sit back, relax, and gather your thoughts before continuing your investigation into the secrets hidden within these walls.
